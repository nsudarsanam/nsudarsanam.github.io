Hi. I'm thoughtfox.

I believe in the beauty of something not belonging. I believe in something being out of place. I believe that through the process of not belonging and being displaced, people redefine what it means to call some place home. They put down roots, they change a place and a place changes with them. I would like to learn how culture, food, music, language and economy change when "the others" come in. I would like to unravel the tangled web of stories that lie behind the deceptively simple facade of a place. That is what thoughtfox is dedicated to.
